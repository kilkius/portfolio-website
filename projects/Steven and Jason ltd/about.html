About us

Steven and Jason's decorating was founded in 2015 in London. Due to reliability and professionality of the staff, the company has now grown into one of the biggest decorating companies in London with over 500 employess and 300 tradesmen.

We know that it is hard to find a company that will redecorate your house exactly how you want it and finish the work on time. Our tradesmen go through a carefully structured course to become the best at what they do. We pride ourselves to training all of our collegues to the highest standard so they can show those skills to the customers who put trust in our company for their decoration needs.
